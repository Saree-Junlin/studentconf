What is This Symposium?

The 2023 Postgraduate Research Symposium on Linguistics and Language Data Analytics(PRSLA), to be collocated with PACLIC 2023 , offers an inter-institutional platform for the postgraduate students sharing interest in linguistics and language data analysis to showcase their research outcome, present and discuss their works, exchange ideas and share experiences. Scholars and experts are also welcomed to submit abstracts. PRSLA(2023) will last for three days. Prof. Charles YANG and Prof. Lori LEVIN are invited to give seminar series to help the attendees identify research topics and address challenging issues.

To give presenters and audience maximum ability to participate in the conference, PRSLA(2023) will be a "Hybrid Mode" Symposium.

Themes (including but not limited to)

  • Corpus Linguistics & Language Resource Construction
  • Computational Linguistics & Natural Language Processing
  • Digital Humanities
  • Cognitive Processing & Neuro-/psycho-linguistics
  • Studies on Low-resource and Endangered languages
  • Studies based on Data Analytics involving any of the following and/or other fields: Large Language Model, Language Acquisition, Language Variations, Emotion, Metaphor, Translation and Interpreting, Conversation Analysis etc.
